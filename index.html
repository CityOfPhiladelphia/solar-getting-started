Getting Started

The next tab in this application shows the results of extensive spatial analyses to assess the suitability of every rooftop in Philadelphia for solar panel installations.

You can type an address in the search bar at the top of the map to zoom to a specific location.

Click this button to turn different map layers on/off, such as License & Inspection permits involving solar construction.

See a map legend explaining what different colors in the map are showing.

The swipe tool can be used to interact with the solar map layer to see other layers below them.

Click this button to switch the basemap to show aerial imagery.

Click on any building footprint to see more detailed information about its solar potential.
